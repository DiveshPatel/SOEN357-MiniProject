iFit Marketplace

A responsive mobile marketplace to sell fitness data.
Made by Dezignz.

Introduction

With society entering the modern age of technology, many companies are harvesting user data to profile users. Companies such as Facebook and Google use this data to target advertisements to attract more advertisers and in turn increase company revenue. But what do users get in return? Nothing! The loss of data is inevitable, so why not make a profit from it? Our solution is to provide the iFit Marketplace, a personal marketplace for users to sell their personal fitness data, and gain a profit from it.

Solution

Our team at Dezignz has been hired by FreeYourData to provide a marketplace solution for users to sell their personal fitness data. Our solution is to design a simple and intuitive mobile app for users to easily sell their fitness data. Our goal is to make the process of selling data as simple and intuitive as possible. The user interface will be extremely user friendly to a wide variety of users with different technological backgrounds - whether youâ€™re new to technology or an expert. We want people to access the marketplace, view their balance, and sell data in a hassle free way, and consume as little time as possible. We know that many people are invested in their daily activities, and keeping track of their data can become a headache. Our solution will allow users to sell their data and make money within just a few minutes!
